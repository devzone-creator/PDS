= Authentication and authorisation service using OOP

----------------------------------------------------------------

=Steps
    -User knock on register by providing name, email and password as well
    -User details are stored and processed for verification by providing random 6 digit
    -User enters 6 digit and granted access

    ====------------------ Authorisation flow ---------------====
    -=-->A random number is generated and sent to user on the page
    -=-->User then enters and another confirmation is sent to the email with a "YES" and "NO"
    -=-->When User clicks "NO" then authorisation fails whilst
    -=-->When User clicks a "YES" then access is granted and redirected to the page
    -=-->User can log out anytime.
